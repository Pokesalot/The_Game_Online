Welcome to The Game! where you will play cards below into piles, also below.
The top two piles only go up, and the bottom piles only go down!
You can however make them go the wrong way but only by exactly ten at a time!
That is, if the top pile has a 63, you can play any card 64 or higher, or 53.
Get as far as you can. you will unlock more difficult versions as you play which will make things harder at random!
Your score will go up for playing more cards before you end your turn, as well as 5 points every time you reverse!
So zany! GLHF!
